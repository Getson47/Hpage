Who am I

My name is Getson Chirwa and I currently live in Cape Town and I really enjoy working with technology and wanting to learn more about technonolgy.

I enjoy reading books, watching intresting documentaries, watchimg movies, series and giving back to the community.

During the first hard lockdown back in March, we all had free time on our hand to do other activities, I was working in the hospitality industry at the time and due to the travel ban, the hospitality was going through a rough patch jobs were being lost through retrenchement, things were really bad. I was worried about my job so I started doing researh about jobs that will be in demand in the future.

I came across coding, being able to build websites and app, this really facinated me, which lead me to wanting to learn more about this. I did my research and I knew this is something that that I want to do.
